My dream career path

I am entering my second year of Economics and Finance at the University of Surrey and plan to pursue a sandwich placement between my second and third years. My ambition is to secure a placement in Islamic finance, though I recognize the field’s niche nature may make this challenging. Following my undergraduate studies, I aspire to complete a master’s degree in Islamic finance. My goal is to gain valuable experience in the UK finance sector, working in a professional services role for approximately two years, before eventually transitioning to the Middle East to further my career.

I was inspired to go into Islamic finance in year 13. When I was applying to university i came across the dilemma of choosing to get student loans. Due to the interst in these contracts they can be considered againt my religion. I went to do further research to find different methods of abtaining the capital however there was little to no options. This gave me the determination to enter the Islamic Finance sector to help create a system so younger muslims dont have to come across the same dilemma as me.
